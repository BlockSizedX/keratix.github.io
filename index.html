About KeratiX

A protein worth more than we throw away

Human hair is one of the densest natural sources of keratin on earth — and India alone generates tons of it daily with nowhere structured to go. KeratiX collects that waste and runs it through an eco-friendly extraction process with zero harmful discharge, turning it into a 100% natural, high-protein biomaterial.

  • 100% natural — zero harmful chemicals
  • High-protein bio source, pure keratin
  • Sustainable, circular-economy certified process
